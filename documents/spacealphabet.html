The book was created for children between 1st grade and 5th grade, and is fashioned after traditional books meant to teach young children the alphabet. However, our book has a twist. It explains complex and advanced space terminology (such as apoapsis and Delta V) with simple words. I collaborated on the project with one of my best friends, Staś.
