One of Nate’s favorite days during the summer of 2016 occurred while he was in Prague, visiting a friend that had moved there. He and his friend, Filip, had not done much that day. They had mostly played video games and used the computer. Near the end of the day, Filip’s mother began to yell at them to exit the building and go outside, but they had protested that there was not much to do outside. This was until they realized that they both had Pokémon Go! accounts, and then went outside for an hour and a half to play it together. ‘Twas a joyous occasion. Nate was also far better at the game than Filip. That fact contributed to much frustration, annoyance and anger on the part of Filip, which was infinitely more enjoyable than anything that Pokémon Go! had to offer.

Nate enjoys learning about computers, the hardware associated with them, the wider spectrum of electronic devices and technology, science, math, english and design.
