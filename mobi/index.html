Mobi

Designed and built by Jonathan and Nate

The only tablet you'll ever need

The Mobi offers multiple features and accessories such as built-in Airplay, allowing you to do multiple things with your phone at the same time, and extra storage space for anything that you want to store inside the Mobi case.

Along with the order, you will receive multiple adapters for things such as microUSB to Lightning, so that the Mobi can be compatible with as many phones as possible. If the Mobi does not come with the adapter you need, you can use any adapters you may already own.

For the techies in all of us

The Mobi T allows you to customize your Mobi experience. You can augment your Mobi in whatever way you please, such as adding extra storage space to your device, adding extra battery modules, and adding more RAM. The full list of available augmentation chips can be found here.

Its revolutionary design allows you to customize your device like no product has ever let you customize your device before. Because if you own something, you should be able to make it your own.
