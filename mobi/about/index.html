Your phone becomes your tablet

The Mobi provides an increase of resolution to almost astronomical proportions, meaning high-definition screens no matter what resolution you started out with.

It has a refresh rate of 90Hz, ensuring a lag-free experience while you use your Mobi.

Thanks to the large resolution and the high refresh rate, the Mobi is a great addition to your tech arsenal whether you’re on the go, or at home.

It works like a charm

To put your phone into the Mobi, push the case lock button and open the screen. Then attach the adapter you need for your phone, slide the phone in, and voilà! You've got a much bigger screen, so you can squeeze every last drop of functionality and performance out of your phone.

The Mobi can fit most phones on the market, up to phablet sizes, so you can purchase a Mobi without fear that your particular make or model of phone won't fit. The maximum supported phone dimensions are available here.

It comes with a large internal battery, which automatically charges your phone, so you can keep playing that game or watching that movie without the constant fear of running out of battery.

You can use it however you want

The Mobi offers built-in Airplay, so as long as you've got a phone that supports it, you can let your kids play a video game while you watch a movie somewhere else in your house, all using the same phone.

You can also slide your phone into the Mobi, and suddenly have access to a much larger screen. Whether you're watching movies or videos, at home or with friends, whatever you do with your Mobi, it's up to the task.

Hackers, rejoice.

The Mobi T is customizable because you have the opportunity to make whatever changes you may want to your phone, whether it’s more RAM, battery life, extra storage space, extra processing power or extra graphics power.

You get one free augmentation chip with your Mobi T order, and then after that you have the freedom to choose from a wide variety of augmentation chips to really customize your Mobi experience, the full list of which is available here.
